Meet our exceptional team

With over 150 years of combined experience, we live to make big ideas a reality!


Rob Biedenharn

Rob has over twenty years experience since earning his Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. This includes real-time inventory and warehouse management systems for plastics extrusion and thermoforming companies, retail supply chains in distribution and order fulfillment, and evaluation and integration of both stable and leading-edge technologies into business solutions.

Rob’s talents aren’t limited to technology. From a concentration in economics at MIT and being among the first to receive the APICS Certification in Integrated Resource Management in 1993, to managing a team of IT Business Analysts and studies in project risk management, Rob can speak the language of your business to help improve the bottom line.


Ed Sumerfield

Ed has been developing enterprise solutions for 25 years. His primary focus is software and architectural excellence with a specialty in Agile development techniques.

Ed has the ability to work with teams as a member or take on the leadership role as is required to produce the best results for the business. A cooperative driving force, Ed has proven his ability to produce results time and time again.


Doug Alcorn

Doug has been a professional software developer for more than 15 years, with five of those years doing web development with Rails. He has both participated in and led teams using SCRUM, XP, and several variations in between.

Doug believes in and practices a well developed discipline of test first development, and has extensive experience working with startup ventures and Fortune 500 companies.


Dewayne Greenwood

Dewayne has 10+ years combined entrepreneurial, business development, and finance experience. He made the shift toward web application development in 2007, to which he has been working within agile development teams ever since.

Dewayne is also respected for his years of entrepreneurial experience, including the launch and direction of business and marketing activities for projects like BudgetSketch... a Ruby on Rails web application that now has users in over 100 countries.

These days, he's known around Gaslight as the design and user experience expert that enjoys creating beautiful, yet highly functional user interfaces. He's also our go-to-guy for solving business and process issues for our clients.


Chris Nelson

Chris has 10+ years experience developing software for startups to Fortune 500 companies. He was a longtime java developer who came the long way around to Ruby on Rails. He is active in the JRuby project and getting the word out about (J)Ruby on Rails wherever, whenever and however he can.

Chris is now working full time in Ruby and having a great time doing it. You'll also find him presenting at various Rails related events around the country.


Bill Barnett

Since earning his bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of Cincinnati in 2002, Bill has developed mostly Web applications first in PHP and later in Ruby for Cincinnati Children's Hospital, SCRIP-SAFE, and dozens of clients mostly in the Cincinnati area. Bill founded the local PHP users group and is an active member in the Cincinnati and Columbus Ruby Brigades as well as the Cincinnati Agile Round Table.

When not practicing his craft, Bill likes to travel, play ice hockey, and feed his entrepreneurial desires providing technical direction at BudgetSketch and business development at Cincinnati Coworks.
